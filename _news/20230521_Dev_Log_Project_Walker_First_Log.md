It has been a long time since we started the development of Project Walker Reimagined (PW), about 1 year and a few months already, and now I think I must start register our progress, I am planning on doing this every two weeks at least.

For people who still didn't heard of PW, it will be a Zombie Apocalypse survival add-on for MC Bedrock (at least initially), it will be a DayZ Standalone™ and S.T.A.L.K.E.R™ styled art and we hope to bring some elements of post-apocalyptic games or similar.

So in the past months we have been working hard on finishing a beta for the add-on and making most of the add-on systems working, all the add-on's gadgets is working, the guns system is almost complete, and the humanoid and animal AI has progressed a lot as well.

A lot of work has been done on our gun system as well, we are using an on_use event and tags, this means that we can make gun DLC's for our add-on in the future.

Our next steps is to polish even more our beta, work more on trying to build a community for the add-on and hopefully create more content, like maps, enemies and etc.

Thanks for reading this, and I hope to see you on our next dev log.
