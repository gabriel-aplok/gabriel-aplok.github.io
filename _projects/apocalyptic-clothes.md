The Apocalypse is coming to Minecraft Bedrock! Apocalyptic Clothes brings apocalyptic-themed clothes to the Minecraft world!

Classic and iconic clothes like the Military Camo, Medic, Ghillie, Ninja and Hazmat!

The Apocalyptic Clothes add-on adds a total of 41 new items to the game. All items are craftable using the stonecutter with a iron ingot.

Known as **"Crafting Dead Clothing"**

## Installation

Open the **.mcpack** with **Minecraft** or extract and copy manually the folder to te **/Android/data/com.mojang.minecraftpe/** behavior and resources in the respective folders.

---

Just make sure you enable the [experimental settings.](#experimental-gameplay) Also make sure you have enabled **<span class="green">BOTH</span>** resources and behavior packs when creating the world.
