<h1>Project Walker</h1>

Explore a run-down, overgrown world taken over by zombies. To survive, you must scavenge for food and weapons, and fight off the zombies!

_This is a reimagining of the Legacy Project Walker, with new ideas, and not following HardSK's art anymore, but Gabriel Aplok's. New systems, new mechanisms, new animations, fluid and smooth, satisfactory sound effects for a nice gameplay! All this in one place!_

**Status:** Under development
