What is "Aplok Guns"?

Survival friendly guns mod with lightweight and beautiful models, satisfying sound effects, animations and much more.

Notice

This mod add guns with low-poly models and does not represent any mode of realism in visual, just focus on animations and sounds, the models are something close to something that mojang would make.
